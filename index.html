About Us

It all started 16 years ago. A fusion of creativity, talent and skill is what Ordin@trix is all about. Ordin@trix has evolved through the years moving from an interschool festival at the state level to an international event. Over the years we have had students from China, Muscat, Japan and other countries as regular participants.

The event has witnessed gracious presence of celebrities like Mr. Amol Gupte and Miss Manju Singh apart from experts from various technical fields. The movies showcased here have been screened at the movie festivals by PVR Cinemas and CHINH India Festival.

Since it's conception, Ordin@trix has come a long way and so have the PYROTECHS. They are the very nuclei of Ordin@trix, who create fireworks in the field of technology. They breathe life into Ordin@trix.

PYROTECH members are an elite team of student computer enthusiasts. They have represented the school in various competitions and won laurels in events such as web designing, software development, image editing, movie making, 3-D modelling, Robotics, photography and others.

Innovate@trix

Vulnerability is the birthplace of innovation creation and change.

Pitching a Startup Idea

Participants: 1 Team consisting of 3 students
Eligibilty: Classes IX-XII
  1. All teams must have a CEO CFO and CTO
  2. The startup could be anything from a social start-up to an FMCG start-up. We expect to see something new, Surprise us!
  3. a broad list of the types of start-ups is given below for your reference. You need not abide by it.
    1. Social Start-ups
    2. Fianancial Start-ups
    3. FMCG start-ups
    4. Tech Start-ups
  4. Referencing to online material for having an idea, proving statistics or any such thing which could be considered as fair means will be allowed however direct plagerism will lead to disqualification

    5. Qualification Round

  5. The qualification round will begin from Thursday 16 July and end on Saturday 18 July'20.
    Top 10 teams will be shortlisted for the final round on Saturday 25 July 2020.
  6. Participants have to create a powerpoint presentation explaining their startup idea.
  7. The powerpoint must have a max. of 10 slides covering the following aspects:
    1. Executive Summary
    2. Target Market
    3. Competitors
    4. Operations
  8. All participants must submit their ppts by mail to the event heads latest by 18th July 2020.
  9. The list of qualifiers for the final round would be uploaded on the Ordin@trix website on 20 July after 12 noon.

    10. Final Round

  10. Participants must create a commercial for their product along with a company name and logo:
    1. Use of templates is not allowed.
    2. Any qualifying team found using templates shall be disqualified.
  11. Participants will
    1. Pitch their start-up company to the investors on a web conferencing platform.
    2. Be given 10 minutes to present their complete idea including the commercial, financial projections and marketing plan.
  12. The above will be followed by:
    1. A round of cross questioning on several aspects.
    2. Participants' efforts to convince the judges to invest in their company.

    Judgement Criteria:

    • Presentation of Idea
    • Marketing Strategy
    • Feasability / Ease of execution
    • Social Appeal

Website Designing + Image Editing + Movie Editing + Music Editing

Participants: 1 Team consisting of 2 students
Eligibilty: Classes VI-XII
  1. This creative event will comprise web development, video editing and digital imaging. The teams may add other creative inputs too.
  2. PROMPT:- Create a landing page for a new video editing software of your own
    1. You will need to design the software’s logo and all the media required
    2. The participants may create a introductory movie for their software
    3. you could include features like discussion board etc. for users to discuss their queries
    4. We expect an interactive and user friendly interface to make the webpage pleasing to the eye.
  3. Use of templates is strictly prohibited.
  4. All text editors are allowed.
  5. The use of external libraries such as bootstrap, jquery, etc. is allowed.
  6. All teams must send their entries in the form of a zip folder or share the link to their github repositories, latest by 25th July.
  7. There is no second round of the event . Entries will be judged by an expert jury and results shall be announced.

    8. Judgement Criteria:

    • Originality
    • Aesthetics
    • User Friendliness
    • Multimedia Projection
    • Backend

Photography Event

Participants: 1 Team consisting of 1 student
Eligibilty: Classes VI-XII
  1. The theme for the event is - 'Happiness'.
  2. Participants would have to depict a story line with the help of 3 pictures clicked on the theme given above.
  3. Pictures must be compiled into a collage with a JPG/JPEG format.
  4. Short description and topic of the story must also be given along with the collage.
  5. Minimal editing / in-camera editing is allowed on the images clicked.
  6. Participants must also provide the original photographs along with the edited photograph. The original photographs must be named 'Original1', 'Original2' and 'Original 3'.
  7. The entries should be shared via email with the event heads latest by Monday, 20 July 2020.
  8. The participants will present their compilations in front of the judges on Saturday, 25 July 2020.
  9. The details to join the video conferencing will be shared via email.

    10. Judgement Criteria:

    • Relevance to Topic
    • Aesthetics
    • Originality of Pictures
    • Storyline
